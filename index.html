About This System

This is a basic navigation system of GCTU to help students, lecturers, staff and vistors navigate around the school building. It includes a map and direction features.
This map has a total area of 47,989.04 m² (516,549.69 ft²) and a total distance of 894.11 m (2,933.43 ft).
